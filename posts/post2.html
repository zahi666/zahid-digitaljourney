iPhone 17 Pro Max: What’s New & Is It Worth the Upgrade?

Published: 2025-10-07

The iPhone 17 Pro Max launched in September 2025, and it’s turning heads. With powerful specs, improved battery life, new camera features, and better connectivity, it promises a lot. But is it really worth upgrading from older models? Let’s dive in.

Key specs & features

  • Powerful chipset and improved RAM/storage options.
  • ~6.9-inch OLED ProMotion display with 120Hz refresh.
  • Battery improvements and optimized power delivery.
  • Camera upgrades with high-res sensors and better low-light performance.

If you're using a much older iPhone, the upgrade makes sense. But if you already own a recent Pro/Pro Max, evaluate which features matter to you before buying.
